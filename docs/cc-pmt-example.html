Recommendations for a Good Credit Score

  • Payment History (35% of your score): Always pay on time. Even one late payment can cause significant damage.
  • Credit Utilization (30% of your score): Keep your balance low. Aim to use less than 30% of your Credit Limit (e.g., if limit is $5,000, keep balance below $1,500).
  • Pay in Full: To avoid Interest charges and maximize your score, pay the Total New Balance every month.
  • Length of History: The longer you keep accounts open and in good standing, the better.
